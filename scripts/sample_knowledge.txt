MACHINE LEARNING ENGINEER JOB REQUIREMENTS

Typical Requirements:
- 5+ years of ML engineering experience
- Expert in Python and at least one ML framework (PyTorch or TensorFlow)
- Experience deploying ML models to production
- Strong understanding of MLOps, CI/CD for ML
- Experience with distributed training (Ray, Spark, or similar)
- Knowledge of feature stores and model serving infrastructure
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)

Nice to Have:
- Experience with Large Language Models (LLMs) and Generative AI
- Published research papers or technical blog posts
- Experience with vector databases and embedding models
- Knowledge of A/B testing and experimentation frameworks
- Experience with real-time ML systems

COMPANY CULTURE SIGNALS
- Engineering-driven decision making
- Strong emphasis on code review and testing
- Remote-friendly work environment
- Continuous learning and conference attendance supported
